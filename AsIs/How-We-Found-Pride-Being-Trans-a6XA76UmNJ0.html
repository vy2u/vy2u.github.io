How We Found Pride Being Trans

2015-08-23
being trans is an amazing gift I know for me the biggest key to finding a sense of pride and being trans has been finding other trans people who I genuinely enjoy it is the bravery and resilience of my community that makes me feel proud to be trans one of the things that makes me really proud is seeing the effect that I have on my family and the people around me my little sister who's seen me as a trans person almost all of her life when she went away to college she was in an all-girls dorm and there was a young guy there that was trans and she basically got all the people around her to treat him like a human being and create a space for him to be safe when my mother finally for Mother's Day this year 2015 she said you have challenged my perception of what it is to be a woman and I embrace you fully daughter learning about trans history made me proud to be trans marching and doing demonstrations for trans people made me feel really amazing when I went to the White House and spoke up on behalf of my transgender undocumented community I was the co-creator of a project called the trans 100 which is an annual list celebrating 100 out trans activists the most proud of ever felt was at our first live event which was completely done by trans people for trans people I have been fortunate to be a part of TV shows that I would have never thought when I was younger that I would even be given an opportunity let alone an acting opportunity I started printing a magazine called original plumbing the first issue we didn't know how was going to go and we ended up selling out in a few days I think that it is exciting to be part of a gender revolution and a time of that happening and I get to be part of that history learn to love yourself as a trans person because you are changing the
