DIY Rose Oil for Skin, Hair, Nails

2016-02-10
with Valentine's Day coming up I thought I'd share this DIY rose oil as a fun way to preserve your Valentine's roses for much longer or maybe this recipe will be a good excuse for you all to go out and buy yourself some roses why not you deserve it this rose oil can be used for moisturizing and pampering your body hair and nails it's all natural and super easy to make first heat up a pot of water while you wait for the water to boil pick 2 to 3 organic roses to prepare one cup of rose petals then have some fun shredding those rose petals once the water is boiling turn off the stove pour one cup of grapeseed oil into a glass jar you can also use jojoba or olive oil if you want add the petals into the oil and then use a wooden utensil to swirl them around next cover the jar and place it into the hot water warming the oil will help release the scent from those petals when the water cools down completely remove the jar then let the jar sit for at least 24 hours leave it in longer for stronger results this next step is optional use a sieve to separate the rose petals from the oil and it's good to go personally I prefer to keep the rose petals in the oil I'm even adding fresh petals because I want to let them sit and make the oil stronger over time I hope you liked this DIY rose oil recipe please give this video a thumbs up if you did I also made another video showing you how to make rose water which is so much easier to make the link is in the description below thanks for watching I love you all so much and I hope you all have a beautiful Valentine's weekend bye
